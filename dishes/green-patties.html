Green Patties!

Description:

The nature's larder is bursting and the wild plants - the weeds - stand knee high. You can actually eat your weeds. It's good for you - and your wallet. Here's a recipe for some tasty vegetarian patties full of vitamins. those are the green patties:

Ingredients

  • 1 basket full of different fresh herbs like e.g. stinging nettle, garlic
  • mustard, wild garlic, goutweed, dandelion leaves, chickweed, birch leaves and chervil
  • 2 chopped onions
  • 3 chopped cloves of garlic
  • 2 eggs
  • 1.5 cup wheatmeal. You can replace some of it with oatmeal
  • A little milk
  • Salt
  • Butter for frying
  • A Shield

Steps

  1. Wash and drain the weeds and chop them finely
  2. Mix them with onion, wheatmeal, milk, eggs and season with salt
  3. The consistency must be quite firm
  4. Scoop a spoonful of the mixture and shape into a ball using the balm of your hand
  5. Melt the butter in a frying pan and cook the patties until golden brown on both sides
