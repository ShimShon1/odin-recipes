The Viking Fish Soup!

Description:

The Vikings had quick access to the Wadden Sea, and so fish and shellfish were an important supplement to the diet and a great source of protein. this is the Hvidinggaard fish soup:

Ingredients

  • 5 carrots
  • 5 medium sized onions
  • Butter
  • Water ca. 1.5 litre
  • Chervil
  • Cress
  • 1 cup cream
  • Salt
  • 750 g cleaned fish eg. salmon or trout
  • Finely chopped top of spring onion
  • A sword

Steps

  1. Clean and dice the onions and carrots. Rinse the herbs and chop medium fine
  2. Melt the butter in a cooking pot
  3. Add the onions and carrots and cook for a couple of minutes.
  4. Leave to simmer until the vegetables are 'al dente'
  5. It's now time to add the herbs and the cream
  6. Cut the fish in bite-sized pieces and add them to the soup
  7. Let it simmer over low heat until the fish is tender
  8. Season to taste
  9. Sprinkle with chopped spring onion and serve with bread
